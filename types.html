Types of Phishing Attacks

Email Phishing

The most common type, where attackers send mass emails impersonating legitimate organizations to trick recipients into revealing sensitive information or clicking malicious links.

Spear Phishing

A targeted form of phishing aimed at specific individuals or organizations, often using personalized information to increase credibility.

Whaling

Phishing attacks that target high-profile individuals like executives or business leaders, often involving more sophisticated and convincing tactics.

Smishing and Vishing

Phishing attempts carried out via SMS (smishing) and voice calls (vishing), respectively, to deceive individuals into providing sensitive information.

Pharming

Redirecting users from legitimate websites to fraudulent ones by exploiting vulnerabilities in DNS servers or the user's device.

Clone Phishing

Creating a replica of a legitimate email with malicious links or attachments to deceive the recipient into thinking it's authentic.
