About Phishing

What is Phishing?

Phishing is a cyberattack technique that involves tricking individuals into providing sensitive information by masquerading as a trustworthy entity. These attacks are typically carried out through deceptive emails, messages, or websites.

Objectives of Phishing Attacks

  • Stealing Credentials: Gaining access to usernames and passwords.
  • Financial Fraud: Obtaining credit card details or banking information.
  • Data Breaches: Accessing sensitive organizational data.
  • Malware Distribution: Infecting systems with malicious software.
